BallCoacher Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

1. Overview

BallCoacher ("we", "our", "the App") is a tactical football coaching tool developed by Ofir Amedy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account information: Email address used for authentication (via Supabase Auth).
  • Tactical data: Formations, scenarios, player positions, and coaching notes you create within the App. This data is stored in our cloud database to enable sync across sessions.
  • Images/video frames: When you use the Video Analysis feature, selected frames are sent to an AI service (Anthropic Claude) for player position detection. We do not store these images on our servers.
  • Device information: Basic device identifiers required by the platform for authentication and session management.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or analytics SDKs.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing.
  • We do not track your activity across other apps or websites.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • To save and sync your tactical scenarios and coaching data.
  • To process video frames for AI-powered player detection (frames are sent to Anthropic's API and not retained).

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: Authentication and database hosting. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Video frame analysis for player detection. Anthropic Privacy Policy

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase's infrastructure with Row Level Security (RLS) policies. Authentication tokens are stored locally using secure device storage (Expo SecureStore). All network communication uses HTTPS encryption.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your tactical data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete your scenarios and coaching data at any time within the App. To request full account deletion, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

BallCoacher is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date at the top.
