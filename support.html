Getting Started

BallCoacher is an advanced tactical football coaching board. Place players on the pitch, analyze formations, run tactical simulations, and use AI-powered video analysis to detect player positions from match footage.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my formations?

Tap the Scenarios option in the control bar to save and load your tactical setups. Scenarios sync automatically to the cloud when you're signed in.

How does Video Analysis work?

Import a match frame or video, and AI will detect player positions on the pitch. You can then adjust positions manually and apply them to the tactical board.

Which devices are supported?

BallCoacher is optimized for iPad in landscape orientation for the best coaching experience.

How do I change the language?

Tap your profile icon in the top bar and select "Language" to choose from 9 supported languages.
